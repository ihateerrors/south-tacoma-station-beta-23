South Tacoma Station Access Improvements

Thank you

Thanks for your interest in the South Tacoma Station Access Improvements project.

This online open house closed on Jan. 9, 2024. 

Next steps

We will continue to finalize the environmental review and refine designs for the proposed changes throughout the project area. In 2024, the Sound Transit Board will select the improvements to be built.

All improvements are set to be completed by 2030.
